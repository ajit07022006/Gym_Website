Chest & Triceps

The Incline Bench Press Targets the upper chest and shoulders, enhancing muscle definition and strength. It’s performed on an incline bench using a barbell or dumbbells, emphasizing the upper pectorals and anterior deltoids.

Dumbbell Flys A chest isolation exercise that stretches and contracts the pectoral muscles. It enhances chest width and muscle control, performed with dumbbells on a flat or incline bench.

Push-Ups A bodyweight exercise working the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Variations like wide or diamond push-ups target different muscle groups for strength and endurance.

Cable Crossovers Uses cables for continuous tension on the chest muscles. This movement enhances muscle contraction, improving chest shape and definition.

Skull Crushers (EZ Bar) Primarily targets the triceps' long head. Performed with an EZ bar while lying on a bench, extending the arms to strengthen and define the triceps.

Overhead Rope Extension (Cable) A triceps-focused cable exercise that improves arm definition and strength by engaging the triceps’ long head through an overhead extension movement.

Dumbbell Kickbacks An isolation exercise for triceps toning. It involves extending the arm backward with a dumbbell while maintaining a bent-over posture.

Reverse Grip Tricep Pushdowns Targets the triceps’ medial head using a reverse grip on a cable machine. This movement helps build balanced tricep strength and muscle definition.
