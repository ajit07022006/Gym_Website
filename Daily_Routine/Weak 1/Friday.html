Chest & Triceps

The Pull-Ups (Weighted)A compound exercise targeting the upper back, biceps, and shoulders. Adding weight increases intensity, building strength and muscle mass.

T-Bar Rows A back-focused movement that strengthens the middle and lower traps, lats, and rhomboids. It enhances thickness and overall back development.

Single-Arm Dumbbell Row Targets the lats and upper back. Performed by pulling a dumbbell towards the torso while stabilizing on a bench, improving muscle balance.

Face Pulls (Cable) Uses a cable machine to strengthen the rear delts, traps, and rotator cuffs, enhancing shoulder stability and posture.

Zottman Curls A unique bicep exercise combining a standard curl with a reverse grip on the way down, working both biceps and forearms effectively.

Incline Dumbbell Curls Performed on an incline bench, isolating the biceps for a deeper stretch and improved peak muscle contraction.

Rope Curls A cable-based biceps exercise using a rope attachment. It enhances grip strength and targets both the short and long heads of the biceps.

Drag CurlsA variation of bicep curls where the barbell is pulled close to the body, minimizing front deltoid involvement and emphasizing biceps growth.
